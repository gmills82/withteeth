With Teeth Films

Our Mission

We make accessible movies designed to provoke a reaction.

Provocative Content

We recognize that people around the world are hungry for original content that's provocative, fun and a little dangerous.

Nail-biting experiences

People want to be surprised. People want to be frightened. People haven't been getting what they want.

Films that meet you where you are

With increasingly fewer suspense and horror films to choose from (and the rapid advancement of personal media devices), fewer movie watchers are flocking to theaters. Further, people have become less willing to pay the exorbitant costs to see new movies at the local cinema, no matter how good it's suppose to be.

Original content

With Teeth Films produces original movies, delivered on a global platform, that people can afford to access at their convenience. No more lines. No more waiting.

We have something for you to watch, and we're pretty sure you're going to like it.

PRODUCTION STAFF

With Teeth Films is owned and operated by Chris Wiggins and Shannon Pokines. Shannon and Chris have been doing experimental films for the last five years. While both are producing the company's active projects, Shannon's primary focus is acting. Chris writes the team's scripts and will be directing With Teeth Films' first two features, The Passenger and Burns House.
